Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

At Buy Thank You, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Payment Information

When you make a payment through Buy Thank You, we collect:

  • Transaction amount
  • Payment method used (UPI, credit card, debit card, etc.)
  • Transaction ID and timestamp
  • Payment status (successful, failed, pending)

Important: We do NOT store your complete credit card numbers, CVV codes, or banking passwords. All sensitive payment data is handled directly by our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway partners.

1.2 Contact Information

We may collect:

  • Email address (if provided for receipts or notifications)
  • Phone number (if provided for payment verification)

1.3 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you access our service:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website or source

1.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and improve security

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect website functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Payment Processing

  • To process and complete your "Thank You" payments
  • To verify and authenticate transactions
  • To send payment confirmations and receipts

2.2 Communication

  • To send transaction notifications and updates
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To provide customer service

2.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • To monitor for suspicious activity
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse

2.4 Service Improvement

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To conduct research and analytics

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to legal requests and court orders
  • To enforce our Terms and Conditions

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Payment Recipients

When you send a "Thank You" payment, we share the following information with the recipient:

  • Transaction amount
  • Date and time of payment
  • Your email or name (if provided)
  • Any message you include with the payment

3.2 With Payment Processors

We work with third-party payment gateway providers (such as Razorpay, UPI, etc.) to process transactions. These providers have their own privacy policies and security standards. We only share information necessary to complete your transaction.

3.3 With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us with:

  • Hosting and infrastructure
  • Analytics and data analysis
  • Customer support
  • Email delivery services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

3.4 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

3.5 What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information for marketing purposes without consent
  • Rent or lease your data to advertisers

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • PCI-DSS Compliance: Payment card data is handled by PCI-DSS compliant payment processors
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and updates
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to personal information

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Transaction Data: Retained for 7 years for accounting, tax, and legal compliance purposes
  • Account Information: Retained until you request deletion or close your account
  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 2 years for analytics purposes

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access and update your personal information. Contact us to request your data or make corrections.

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements. Transaction records may need to be retained for compliance purposes.

6.3 Opt-Out of Communications

You can unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or contacting us directly. Note that you will still receive transactional emails related to your payments.

6.4 Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt-out of certain tracking technologies.

  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
