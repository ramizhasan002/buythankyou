Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Buy Thank You allows users to send monetary appreciation. Please note the following regarding refunds and cancellations:

1. Non-Refundable Payments

All appreciation payments are voluntary and non-refundable under normal circumstances. Once a payment is made, it cannot be reversed.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered only for the following situations:

  • Technical Errors: If a payment is processed incorrectly due to a verified system error.
  • Duplicate Payments: Accidental duplicate payments confirmed by our support team.

3. Requesting a Refund

  • Contact us within 7 days of the transaction at buythankyou.service@gmail.com.
  • Provide your transaction ID and details of the issue.
  • Our team will review your request and respond within 3–5 business days.

4. Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be returned to the original payment method.
  • Refunds may take up to 10 business days depending on the bank or payment processor.

5. Support

If you have any questions or issues regarding payments, please contact us at:

Email: buythankyou.service@gmail.com

Website: https://buythankyou.onrender.com
